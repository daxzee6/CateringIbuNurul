Tentang Kami

Short History

Catering Ibu Nurul pioneered their culinary journey from a small warung to a leading catering company. Initially just a small warung in a residential neighborhood, Ibu Nurul with her exceptional culinary skills managed to attract customers with her exquisite cuisine. Within a short period of time, her small warung gained wide acceptance from loyal customers who appreciated the quality and consistency of the cuisine offered.

As demand grew, Catering Ibu Nurul began to expand their menu and services. They provide a wide selection of dishes from traditional to international cuisines, as well as cater for various special events such as weddings and corporate events. This transformation brought them to the status of a professional catering company with high quality standards.
